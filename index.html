Latest Projects

TCDSG: Temporally Consistent Dynamic Scene-Graph Generation

We present TCDSG, an innovative end-to-end framework that detects, tracks, and links subject-object relationships across time, generating action tracklets, temporally consistent sequences of entities and their interactions.

DDS: Decoupled Dynamic Scene-Graph Generation Network

In this project, we explore how a decoupled network design can improve performance when dealing with compositional scenarios. In this case, compositional means the network has seen parts of a triplet <subject, object, relationship> individually during training and we aim to predict a new combination during testing.
